For the people who enjoy the thrill, this is a collection of our most exhilirating and popular rides you might want to try during your visit!!

El Toro

Voted #1 in USA Today’s 10 Best Awards in 2022, this award-winning coaster combines the aesthetics of a classic wooden ride with the smooth thrill of ultra-modern technology making it one of the best wooden roller coasters in the country.

The Joker

After strapping into your 4-D wing vehicle, you’ll feel THE JOKER’s evil plan takes shape as you shoot up 12 stories, zooming over hills and pivoting forward with only the sky to catch you. These seats were designed with a one-of-a-kind magnetic technology which allows you to swivel and free-fly with weightless effort.

Jersey Devil Coaster

One of the fastest single-rail coasters in the world, this deranged, thrilling ride is reserved for daredevils only.

Medusa

Journey back to the Gold Rush era and experience the rise and fall of Exile Canyon, an outpost so unforgiving , it was believed to be cursed by Medusa, a mythical gorgon with snakes for hair. Do you have what it takes to escape her wrath in this serpentine floorless roller coaster?

Nitro

This over-the-top coaster goes all out in speed, height and airtime, officially classifying it as a Hypercoaster. This intense breed of thrill is known for the most heart-pounding rides, and Nitro is no exception.
